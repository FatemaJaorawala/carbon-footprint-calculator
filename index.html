Welcome to the Carbon Footprint Calculator

What is Carbon Footprint?

Your carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases you produce, directly and indirectly, through your daily activities. Our app helps you track and reduce your carbon footprint.

How It Works

Answer a few simple questions about your lifestyle, and we’ll calculate your carbon footprint. You’ll also get tips on how to reduce it!
